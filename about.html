Hi, I'm Crystal

Cosmetic tattoo artist, trainer, and the face behind Crystal Cosmetics on the Gold Coast.

Start Your Beauty Journey

Waking Up Ready, Without the Routine

If you're tired of redrawing the same brow shape every morning, or watching your lip colour fade by lunch, that's exactly the daily grind I set out to solve. Cosmetic tattooing exists so you can skip the routine altogether and still feel put together, every single day.

Embracing the Journey

From Sephora Counter to Cosmetic Tattoo Studio

My path here wasn't a straight line. I spent years in fashion and beauty retail, including time behind the counter at Sephora, before the quiet of the COVID lockdowns gave me space to reconsider what I wanted next. I'd always been drawn to the technical, detail-driven side of beauty, so I took the leap into cosmetic tattooing training. What started as a new skill turned into a specialism in lip blush and brow work, and eventually into training other artists myself. Crystal Cosmetics is the studio I always pictured — a place built around client confidence, not just treatments.

A Lifelong Love of Beauty

It Started With My Mum's Makeup Box

Long before this was a career, it was a childhood habit — sneaking into my mum's makeup, experimenting on my cousins, never quite able to leave a face untouched. That same instinct is what still drives every appointment: helping someone feel like the most put-together version of themselves, without needing to think about it.
