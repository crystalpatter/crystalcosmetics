Privacy Policy

Crystal Cosmetics ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

What we collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and payment details when you book a service, purchase a product, sign up to our newsletter, or contact us directly.

How we use it

  • To process bookings, orders and payments
  • To communicate with you about appointments, orders, or enquiries
  • To send marketing communications, where you have opted in
  • To improve our services and website

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted service providers needed to run our business — for example, our booking platform and payment processor — and only to the extent required for them to perform their service.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@crystalcosmetics.com.au.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and understand site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
