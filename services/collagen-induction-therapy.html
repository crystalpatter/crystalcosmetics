Collagen Induction Therapy

Skin needling that stimulates your own collagen production for smoother, firmer, more radiant skin.

Revitalise & Rejuvenate

Let Your Skin Heal Itself

Collagen Induction Therapy (CIT), also known as skin needling, creates controlled micro-channels in the skin to trigger your body's own healing and collagen production — softening the appearance of scarring, fine lines, and uneven texture over a course of sessions.

How It Works

01
Consultation
We sit down together first — your preferred shape, the technique that suits you, and a colour that complements your natural tone.
02
The Procedure
Pigment is matched to your skin undertone and natural colouring. Every decision, from final design to shade, stays in your hands.
03
Comfort First
A topical anaesthetic keeps discomfort to a minimum throughout, with minimal swelling and virtually no scabbing during healing.
04
Follow-Up
Around 4–6 weeks later we revisit the result together, refining shape and topping up pigment wherever the healing process calls for it.
