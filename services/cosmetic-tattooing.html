How It Works

Consultation

Before Your Appointment

How to Prepare

01
Eat First
Keeping blood sugar stable helps you stay comfortable throughout the appointment.
02
Skip Alcohol
Avoid alcohol for 24 hours beforehand to reduce sensitivity or bleeding risk.
03
Avoid Blood Thinners
Ibuprofen, aspirin and fish oil can thin the blood — hold off for 72 hours prior.
04
Prep Your Skin
Come in sun-safe, and avoid recent waxing or tinting near the treatment area.
